## 11) FAQ

### Section Title
**Questions before you start?**

### Section Subtitle
**Here are the answers customers usually want before choosing their first kit.**

### FAQ 1
**Are these kits beginner-friendly?**  
Yes. Many of our kits are suitable for first-time builders, and we recommend starting with designs that feature clear assembly flow and approachable detail levels.

### FAQ 2
**What’s included in the box?**  
Each kit includes the core building materials and instructions you need for assembly. Product pages will show exact contents and any tool or glue notes where relevant.

### FAQ 3
**How long does assembly take?**  
Build time varies by design and detail level, but most kits are best enjoyed as a relaxed, hands-on project rather than a quick craft.

### FAQ 4
**What if a part is missing or damaged?**  
We offer parts replacement support to help you complete your build with confidence.

### FAQ 5
**Can I give this as a gift?**  
Absolutely. Our kits are especially popular as gifts for book lovers, crafters, collectors, and anyone who enjoys thoughtful, display-worthy hobbies.

### FAQ 6
**Do the kits light up?**  
Many of our most-loved kits include lighting features that add warmth and atmosphere once the build is complete. Check individual product pages for details.
