# ROCOXIA — Shopify Section Handoff

Based on the latest refined preview:
- https://roolin89.github.io/booknookworld-redesign-preview/rocoxia/preview.html

Goal:
Translate the Rocoxia homepage redesign into a Shopify-ready section plan for implementation.

---

## 1. Recommended Homepage Structure

1. Announcement Bar
2. Header / Navigation
3. Hero Banner (Image Banner)
4. Trust Bar (Multicolumn / Icon Row)
5. Collection List
6. Featured Collection (Best Sellers)
7. Image With Text
8. Multicolumn Value Props
9. Testimonials
10. FAQ
11. Email Signup
12. Footer

---

## 2. Brand Role of Rocoxia

Rocoxia should function as:
- a brand-forward homepage
- a curated best-seller storefront
- a paid-traffic-friendly landing environment
- a stronger gift / display / atmosphere-led site

It should not feel like:
- an endless product catalog
- a second copy of Book Nook World
- a cluttered homepage with too many mixed goals

---

## 3. Section-by-Section Shopify Plan

---

## Section 1 — Announcement Bar
### Shopify section type
- Announcement bar

### Purpose
- Keep reassurance visible without creating discount clutter

### Recommended content
Use one fixed line only:
- Free Shipping on All Orders · 30-Day Returns · Parts Replacement Support

### Notes
- Avoid repeating multiple discount bars
- Avoid promo spam at the top of the site
- This area should support trust, not overwhelm the user

---

## Section 2 — Header / Navigation
### Shopify section type
- Header
- Mega menu (if needed)

### Recommended main navigation
- Best Sellers
- Book Nook Kits
- Miniature Houses
- Wooden Puzzles
- Support

### Utility navigation
- Search
- Cart

### Notes
- Keep Rocoxia navigation shorter than Book Nook World
- Rocoxia should feel curated, not broad and overwhelming

---

## Section 3 — Hero Banner
### Shopify section type
- Image banner

### Purpose
- Explain the product category quickly for cold traffic
- Establish brand tone and primary conversion path

### Eyebrow
Curated DIY Worlds with Display Value

### H1
Build a tiny world worth displaying.

### Body Copy
Discover curated DIY book nook kits, miniature houses, and wooden puzzles designed for calming hands-on creativity, thoughtful gifting, and shelf-worthy atmosphere.

### Primary CTA
- Shop Best Sellers

### Secondary CTA
- Explore Book Nook Kits

### Supporting points
1. Gift-Worthy — A memorable choice for book lovers, collectors, and creative hobby fans.
2. Atmosphere-First — Warm lighting, intricate detail, and shelf presence are part of the appeal.
3. Curated Selection — Less catalog noise, more products worth starting with.

### Image direction
- Use one strong lifestyle / atmospheric product image
- Prefer shelf, desk, or warm display context
- Do not use a rotating slideshow for the hero

### Notes
- Hero must do more work on Rocoxia than on Book Nook World because the domain name is less category-explicit
- Keep the first screen focused and commercial

---

## Section 4 — Trust Bar
### Shopify section type
- Multicolumn
- Icon row / icon-with-text (depends on theme)

### Purpose
- Build confidence immediately after the hero

### Recommended items
- Curated — Best-selling DIY worlds
- Giftable — For readers, collectors, and creatives
- Display — Shelf-worthy finished results
- Support — Returns and parts replacement

### Notes
- Keep copy short
- If the theme allows icons, use subtle icons only
- This should feel premium, not promotional

---

## Section 5 — Collection List
### Shopify section type
- Collection list

### Purpose
- Give users a clean entry into the main product families without turning the homepage into a full catalog

### Recommended section title
Shop by what you want to feel, build, and display

### Recommended section subtitle
Rocoxia should not feel like an endless catalog. This section helps users enter the site through clear, curated collection paths.

### Recommended collection cards
#### 1. Book Nook Kits
Display-led bookshelf inserts with atmosphere, story, and shelf presence.

#### 2. Miniature Houses
Fantasy and lifestyle builds for customers who want decorative detail and gift value.

#### 3. Wooden Puzzles
Mechanical projects for shoppers drawn to movement, craftsmanship, and display appeal.

### Notes
- Keep this section to 3 cards or 4 maximum
- Do not stack too many collection choices on the homepage
- This is for guided entry, not for full catalog exposure

---

## Section 6 — Featured Collection
### Shopify section type
- Featured collection

### Purpose
- Move high-conviction products earlier in the homepage flow
- Help paid traffic reach likely conversion products faster

### Recommended title
Start with the products people love most

### Recommended subtitle
Move best sellers higher. Give cold traffic a shorter path to products that already carry the strongest emotional and visual proof.

### Recommended collection source
- Homepage Best Sellers
or
- Best Sellers

### Recommended number of items
- Desktop: 4
- Mobile: 2 to 4 swipe cards

### Recommended card structure
- Product image
- Badge
- Product title
- Short descriptor
- Price
- CTA

### Recommended CTA
- View Product

### Recommended badges
- #1 Gift Pick
- Atmosphere Favorite
- Miniature Favorite
- Mechanical Pick

### Notes
- Do not use too many homepage products
- Rocoxia should feel selected
- Keep image proportions consistent

Why Rocoxia

Tiny worlds with atmosphere, detail, and shelf presence

## Section 8 — Multicolumn Value Props
### Shopify section type
- Multicolumn

### Purpose
- Give the user 3 clear reasons to trust the brand and continue shopping

### Recommended title
Why customers choose Rocoxia

### Recommended subtitle
Use this section to give cold traffic a clean reason to trust the brand and keep moving deeper into the site.

### Recommended columns
#### 1. Gift-worthy by design
A thoughtful option for book lovers, fantasy fans, collectors, and creative hobby shoppers.

#### 2. Built for display
These are not just kits to finish — they are decorative pieces meant to stay on shelves and desks.

#### 3. Curated over crowded
The homepage should feel selected and premium instead of overloaded and hard to browse.

### Notes
- This section should be user-facing, not internal strategy wording
- Keep the copy emotional and useful, not abstract

---

## Section 9 — Testimonials
### Shopify section type
- Testimonials
- Multicolumn cards

### Purpose
- Reinforce buying confidence with fast-scanning proof

### Recommended title
Loved for the build, remembered for the display

### Recommended subtitle
Keep review snippets short and homepage-friendly. Long reviews belong lower or on product pages.

### Suggested review snippets
1. Beautiful once assembled, and even better when placed on a shelf with warm lighting.
2. It feels like a premium gift instead of a generic craft kit.
3. A calming weekend project with a result that actually feels worth keeping.

### Notes
- Use short, strong snippets
- Avoid dumping full-length reviews on the homepage
- PDP pages can carry deeper social proof

---

## Section 10 — FAQ
### Shopify section type
- Collapsible content
- FAQ / accordion

### Purpose
- Remove hesitation before users bounce or postpone purchase

### Recommended title
Questions before you choose your first kit?

### Recommended subtitle
Use the homepage FAQ to remove hesitation, not to replace the support center.

### Recommended questions
1. Are these kits beginner-friendly?
2. What makes Rocoxia different?
3. Are these good as gifts?
4. Do the kits light up?

### Notes
- Keep homepage FAQ to 4–6 questions maximum
- Move long operational questions to support pages

---

## 4. CTA Hierarchy

### Primary CTA
- Shop Best Sellers

### Secondary CTA
- Explore Book Nook Kits
- Browse Curated Picks
- See Why People Buy

### Product CTA
- View Product

### Email CTA
- Get Early Access

### Rule
Do not mix too many verbs such as:
- Add to cart
- Shop now
- View all
- Learn more
- Watch now

Keep the homepage CTA system tighter than Book Nook World.

## 5. Image Direction

### Use more of
- shelf display images
- warm lighting images
- atmospheric product shots
- giftable / premium-looking product visuals

### Use less of
- weak slide repetition
- too many mixed visual styles in one section
- plain merch-style layouts that flatten the brand feel

### Principle
Rocoxia should visually communicate:
- atmosphere
- curation
- premium gift appeal
- display value

## 7. Team Handoff Notes

### For design
- Maintain stronger visual consistency than Book Nook World
- Use fewer, better images
- Keep the brand more premium and selected

### For Shopify implementation
- Use native theme sections where possible
- Avoid extra sliders unless necessary
- Keep mobile layout clean and headline wraps controlled

### For merchandising / ops
- Select 4 high-conviction homepage products only
- Use consistent badge logic
- Keep Rocoxia assortment tighter than Book Nook World homepage

---

## 8. Strategic Reminder

Rocoxia should not compete with Book Nook World by doing the same job.

Rocoxia should be:
- more curated
- more premium in feel
- more ad-traffic-friendly
- more emotionally positioned around gifting, display, and atmosphere
