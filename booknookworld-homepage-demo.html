FAQs

Everything Customers Usually Ask Before Ordering

Are book nook kits beginner friendly?

Yes. Many kits are suitable for beginners and come with guided assembly steps. You can also label products by difficulty to make selection easier.

How long does assembly take?

Most kits take several hours depending on complexity, pace, and experience level. Adding estimated build time on product cards can improve conversion.

Do the kits include lights?

Many book nook kits include LED lighting. Product cards and PDPs should clearly show whether lighting is included.

Are batteries and glue included?

That may vary by product and shipping rules. This should always be clarified in the product description and FAQ.

What if a part is missing or damaged?

Offer a clear replacement support policy. This is a major trust signal for DIY and miniature products.
