Section 7 · FAQ

Conversion-first FAQ for homepage placement

Are these kits beginner-friendly?

Yes. Shopify product cards or collection pages should flag a few recommended starter kits clearly.

What’s included in the box?

Materials, instructions, and feature expectations should be visible before the user reaches the full product page.

How long does assembly take?

Add an estimated time range on collection or PDP templates to lower hesitation.

What if a part is missing or damaged?

Keep parts replacement visible high on the page instead of hiding it in support pages only.
